I am a computer engineering undergad at Northeastern University graduating Spring 2027 advised by

Prof. David M. Rosen , Prof. Michael Everett , and Prof. Hanu Singh.

My reserch interets lie in using geometric structures and physics principles core to robotics and applying them with tools such as optimization and machine learning to improve SLAM and Motion Planning. I believe robotics is at it's best when it is used to explore places difficult for typical human traversal such as in space, underwater, or in the field. I will be applying for PhD's planning for a fall 2027 start.
Besides research I am Northeastern's Marine Robotics team where we compete at the MATE ROV World Championships and at the PEP Electric Boat Racing each year.
